About Zubeda Hassan Sakuru

Leading with Vision, Passion, and Purpose — CEO of Simba Sports Club, Tanzania

Who Is Zubeda Hassan Sakuru?

Zubeda Hassan Sakuru is a visionary Tanzanian business leader and the dynamic Chief Executive Officer of Simba Sports Club. Her leadership blends innovation, inclusivity, and strong governance, making her one of East Africa’s most admired female executives.

She has redefined the way football management works by introducing digital strategies, youth empowerment programs, and community outreach projects that strengthen both the club’s legacy and Tanzania’s sporting culture.

Beyond sports, Zubeda advocates for women’s leadership, talent development, and sustainable transformation — inspiring thousands across Africa.
