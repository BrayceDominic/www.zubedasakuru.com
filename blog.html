Jul 29, 2025 • Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
Signing ceremony of a three year sponsorship deal with BetWay

"This partnership will revolutionise football in Tanzania and reinforce Simba's ambition to become Africa's number one club. The 20 billion investment over three years underscores our unwavering commitment commitment to excellence on the continent. As part of our efforts, we will produce high-quality jerseys, proudly branded with a globally recognized name... As leaders, we are fully committed to this path and our aim is to elevate Simba to the highest standards in both performance and infrastructure." - Zubeda Sakuru

Apr 28, 2025 • Durban, Republic of South Africa
Semi-final qualification press conference following aggregate win against Stellenbosch FC.

"Kauli mbiu yetu sasa hivi ni "HII TUNABEBA" nawapongeza sana w achezaji na benchi la ufundi kwa kazi kubwa waliyoifanya, tunaenda kujipanga sasa kwa fainali na msimu huu tunalichukua hili kombe. Tunaaamini katika uwezo wetu na tumejidhatiti kila siku kuhakikisha tunatimiza malengo ya klabu. Mashabiki wetu hawajabaki nyuma- tunawaomba wahamasike, tuungane na timu katika hatua hii muhimu." - Zubeda Sakuru

Apr 22, 2025 • Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
Announcement of voluntary tax‑payment partnership between Simba SC and Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA)

“Maendeleo yoyote kwenye nchi yanatoka na ulipaji wa kodi. Leo tumeungana na TRA kwa ajili ya kuhamasisha wanachi kulipa kwa hiari, na kupitia michuano hii ya Kombe la Shirikisho tunaamini tutawafikia wengi.Tunatambua kuwa michezo inakusanya watu wengi kwa wakati mmoja, huku soka likiwa ndio kivutio zaidi, kwahiyo tutaendelea kuhamasisha ulipaji wa hiari kwa nguvu zote.” — Zubeda Sakuru.
